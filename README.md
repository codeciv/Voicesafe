# Voicesafe
VoiceSafe is an AI-powered multilingual grievance redressal platform for gig workers. It enables secure voice/text complaint filing, anonymous reporting, real-time case tracking, automated escalation, and platform integration to ensure faster, transparent, and accountable resolution of workplace issues.

# 🛡️ VoiceSafe

### Giving Every Gig Worker a Voice.

---

## 📌 The Problem

India's gig economy is growing rapidly, but the systems protecting gig workers haven't kept pace. Millions of delivery partners and platform workers continue to face delayed payments, unsafe working conditions, harassment, income uncertainty, and limited access to grievance redressal. Existing complaint mechanisms are often fragmented, slow, or inaccessible—especially for workers who are constantly on the move.

---

## 💡 Why We Chose This Problem

Every delivery completed has a worker behind it, yet their concerns often go unheard. We wanted to build a solution that makes reporting issues as simple as placing an order—ensuring every worker has an accessible, transparent, and trustworthy channel to seek help.

---

## 🚀 Our Solution

VoiceSafe is part of the **CRaSH Framework (Competency, Remuneration, Safety & Hybrid Infrastructure)**—a policy-driven approach to improving the welfare of gig workers. It focuses on creating a digital grievance ecosystem that is inclusive, accountable, and easy to use.

---

## 🎙️ What is VoiceSafe?

**VoiceSafe** is an AI-powered multilingual grievance redressal platform designed for gig workers.

### Key Features

* 🎤 Voice & text-based chatbot for complaint filing
* 🌐 Multilingual support for better accessibility
* 🔒 Anonymous or identified reporting
* 🤖 AI-powered issue categorization
* 📍 Real-time complaint tracking
* ⚖️ Automatic escalation to authorities if unresolved
* 🤝 Integration with gig platforms for faster resolution
* ⏰ 24×7 digital accessibility

---

## 🌍 Our Vision

We envision a gig economy where every worker is heard, protected, and treated fairly. By combining AI, digital governance, and worker-first design, VoiceSafe aims to build a more transparent, accountable, and inclusive future of work.
