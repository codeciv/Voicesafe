# Voicesafe
VoiceSafe is an AI-powered multilingual grievance redressal platform for gig workers. It enables secure voice/text complaint filing, anonymous reporting, real-time case tracking, automated escalation, and platform integration to ensure faster, transparent, and accountable resolution of workplace issues.

## 📌 The Problem

With millions of people working for food delivery, ride-hailing, and other gig platforms, India's gig economy is growing faster than ever. But while these workers keep our daily lives running, many still struggle with delayed payments, unsafe working conditions, harassment, and the lack of a simple way to report their problems. Existing grievance systems are often confusing, slow, or simply inaccessible.

---

## 💭 Why We Chose This

While researching the challenges faced by gig workers, we realized that most discussions focus on wages and benefits, but very little attention is given to **how workers can actually raise their concerns**. We wanted to design a solution that makes speaking up simple, accessible, and trustworthy especially for workers who spend most of their day on the road.

---

## 💡 Our Solution 
![CRaSH](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codeciv/Voicesafe/main/voicesafe_prototype_images/crash_framework.png)

VoiceSafe is part of our proposed **CRaSH Framework (Competency, Remuneration, Safety & Hybrid Infrastructure)**. It is a government-backed digital grievance platform that helps gig workers report workplace issues quickly through an AI-powered chatbot available in multiple Indian languages.

---

## 🎙️ Meet VoiceSafe

VoiceSafe allows workers to file complaints using **voice or text**, choose whether to remain **anonymous or identified**, and track the progress of their complaint from start to finish.

### Key Features

* 🎤 Voice & text complaint filing
* 🌐 Multilingual chatbot support
* 🔒 Anonymous and identified reporting
* 🤖 AI-assisted complaint categorization
* 📍 Real-time case tracking
* ⚖️ Automatic escalation of unresolved cases
* 🤝 Integration with gig platforms for timely responses
* ⏰ Available 24×7

---

## 🌱 Our Vision

We believe every gig worker deserves to be heard without fear or unnecessary barriers. VoiceSafe is our attempt to create a system where reporting a problem is easy, transparent, and effective helping build a safer and more accountable gig ecosystem for everyone.
